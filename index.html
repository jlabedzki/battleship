Welcome To Battleship!

OPPONENT

  1. Each player has a 10x10 board on which they can place 5 ships
    • A destroyer (2 squares)
    • A submarine (3 squares)
    • A cruiser (3 squares)
    • A battleship (4 squares)
    • A carrier (5 squares)
  2. Ships can be oriented vertically or horizontally
  3. Each square on the grid corresponds with a coordinate (A-J for columns and 1-10 for rows)
  4. Players take turns firing at a square on the opposing player's grid
    • If the square contains a ship, then the shot is a HIT
    • If the square is empty, then the shot is a MISS
  5. A ship sinks when all the squares for that ship have been marked as HIT
  6. The game ends when one player has sunk all of the opponent's ships
